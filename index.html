About Me

Heyy! I'm Adwaith R — forensic science nerd, part-time wanderer, and full-time PhD struggler at NFSU Gandhinagar.

I kicked things off with a BSc (Hons.) in Forensic Science at Jain University, then dived into an MSc at NFSU,Tripura, where I chose Fingerprints & Questioned Documents (because apparently I enjoy staring at squiggles and smudges).

I’ve cleared UGC NET five times (still no JRF money though — feed me maybe?). I tinker with stuff like MATLAB, Python, LaTeX, Photoshop, Premiere Pro, and Excel when I’m pretending to be productive.

I'm from Kerala — grew up on coconut oil, cinema, and loud opinions — now melting in the heat of Gujarat and trying to stay sane.
