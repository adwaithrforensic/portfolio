About Me

Heyy there, I'm Adwaith R — forensic science nerd, accidental achiever, and currently wrestling with a PhD at National Forensic Sciences University, Gandhinagar.

I started this mad ride with a BSc (Hons.) in Forensic Science from Jain University, then followed it up with an MSc in Forensic Science at NFSU (specializing in Fingerprints and Questioned Documents — because apparently, I love squinting at things).

During my MSc, I messed around with smartphone cameras and PRNU-based sensor identification — because who doesn’t want to play detective with pixels?

I'm fluent in MATLAB, Python, LaTeX, Photoshop, Premiere Pro, Excel, and a bunch of other fancy tools.

Creatively, I shoot videos, click photos, and occasionally make things look cooler than they really are. I play and follow football (Hala Madrid!!!).

I’m from Kerala — born with coconut oil in my hair and cinema in my veins — but these days I’m cooking in the dry heat of Gujarat.
